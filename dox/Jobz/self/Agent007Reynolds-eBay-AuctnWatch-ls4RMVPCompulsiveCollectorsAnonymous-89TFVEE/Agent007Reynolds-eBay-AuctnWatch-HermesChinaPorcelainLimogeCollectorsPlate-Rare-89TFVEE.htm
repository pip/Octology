Rare vintage collector's plate!

Here is a beautiful Hermes limoges china plate, perfect for display or table use.

Based on the design, Ramage by artist Lenke Szechenzyl - this vintage piece was first issued in 1970. It has not been reissued.

Almost flawless - the only thing to report are 4 very slight marks where this plate was held on by a frame. This is shown in the photographs.

Size is 21 cm.

A lovely gift or addition to any collection!
