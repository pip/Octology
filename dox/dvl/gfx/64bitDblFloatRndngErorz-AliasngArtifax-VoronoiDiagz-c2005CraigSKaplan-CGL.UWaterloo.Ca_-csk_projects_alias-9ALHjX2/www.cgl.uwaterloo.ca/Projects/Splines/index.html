Splines Projects in CGL

Last modifed: January 27, 1998
There are several research projects on splines in the computer graphics lab. Although the emphasis of our research is on the mathematics of splines, we test our ideas with protype implementations, and often address issues involving user interfaces, software engineering, etc.

Projects

  • Blossoming, CAGD Algorithms and Software
  • Data fitting
  • Hierarchical Spline Surfaces
  • Polyhedral Splines
  • Surface Pasting
  • Subdivision Surfaces
